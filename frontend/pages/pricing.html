Frequently Asked Questions

How is my delivery price calculated?

Your delivery price consists of a base fee (depending on your chosen plan), a distance fee (cost per mile), and potential surcharges for heavier items or express delivery. Our calculator above can help you estimate costs before booking.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, we pride ourselves on transparent pricing. You'll see the complete breakdown before confirming any delivery. The only additional charges would be for add-on services you specifically request, such as extra insurance coverage.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes! You can choose a different plan for each delivery based on your needs. For drivers, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time, with changes taking effect on your next billing cycle.

How do driver earnings work?

Drivers keep a percentage of each delivery fee based on their subscription level (75% for Standard, 85% for Premium, and 90% for Business Partner). Additional bonuses are available during peak hours, and tips go entirely to drivers.

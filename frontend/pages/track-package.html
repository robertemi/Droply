Tracking FAQ

Where can I find my tracking number?

Your tracking number is provided in the confirmation email you received after booking a delivery. It's also available in your Droply account under "Order History" if you booked while logged in. The format typically looks like: DRP-12345678-RO.

How accurate is the estimated delivery time?

Our estimated delivery times are typically accurate within a 30-minute window for urban areas and a 60-minute window for rural areas. Factors like traffic, weather conditions, and access issues can sometimes affect the exact delivery time.

Can I change the delivery address after shipping?

Yes, for packages that haven't yet been dispatched for final delivery, you can request an address change. Log into your account or contact our customer support team with your tracking number to request this change. Note that additional fees may apply depending on the new destination.

What do the different tracking statuses mean?

Package Processed: Your package has been received and entered into our system.
Departed Facility: Your package has left our distribution center.
In Transit: Your package is on its way to the destination.
Out for Delivery: Your package is on the delivery vehicle for final delivery.
Delivered: Your package has been delivered successfully.

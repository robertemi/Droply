Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Droply.

How much does delivery cost?

Delivery costs are calculated based on distance, package size, and delivery urgency. You'll see the exact price before confirming your delivery request.

How quickly will my package be delivered?

Delivery times vary based on distance and driver availability. You can choose standard delivery (within 24 hours) or express delivery (within 3 hours).

What types of packages can I send?

You can send most packages up to 50 pounds. Prohibited items include illegal goods, hazardous materials, and perishable food items.

How do I become a driver?

Sign up on our platform, complete the verification process including a background check, and once approved, you can start accepting delivery requests.

Are packages insured?

Yes, all packages are automatically insured up to $500. Additional insurance can be purchased for higher-value items.

How do I track my package?

Once your delivery is confirmed, you'll receive real-time tracking updates through our app or website, showing the package's location and estimated arrival time.
